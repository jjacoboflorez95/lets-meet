Cali is the capital of the department of Valle del Cauca and it is the third most populated city in Colombia. It was founded on July 25, 1536 by Sebastián de Belalcázar, which makes it one of the oldest cities in America.

Bogotá is the capital of and largest city in Colombia. It is a place of convergence for people from all around the country and is therefore diverse and multicultural. Within this city, the past and present come together.

Cartagena is a city that is located on the shores of the Caribbean Sea, in the northwestern part of the South American continent. If you are wondering where is Cartagena in Colombia, it is located in the north of the country and is the capital of the Bolívar region
